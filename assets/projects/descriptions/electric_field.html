2D Electric Field Line Simulator

~ Overview ~

This interactive simulation lets you explore electric field lines generated by multiple point charges. Using a real-time engine based on the e2D physics and rendering module, you can visualize how electric fields behave in a dynamic environment.

~ Features ~

You can move charges by dragging them across the canvas, modify their magnitudes with keyboard input, and regenerate field lines either randomly or centered around positive charges. The visualization is smooth, efficient, and customizable.
